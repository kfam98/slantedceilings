What is Slanted Ceilings

Slanted Ceilings is what four students at Cornell University call home next year. The name originated from the four bedroom apartment's distinct ceilings. The ceilings are characterized by their simplistic angles. Unlike other apartments whose ceilings are all but slanted, this 4 bedroom apartment thrives on its uniquesness and will impart wisdom on whoever resides in its space.
